Toad Socks - Shit Creek

Toad Socks began as a side project between three roommates who decided to personify themselves as toads.

The project now lives on as a collaboration between Honey Payton of Sweetest Pot and Bailey Little of Cryhard. Originally centered around hip-hop and rap, Toad Socks' sound has since bent toward synth pop, alternative, and experimental territory.
