panhandle.

Oklahoma City, OK

panhandle. started in a living room in 2023, after Jordan Serio wrote a song processing his move from California to Oklahoma and a friend offered to help record it. Though the project has taken different shapes over time, it's primarily a solo effort, with Brady Bouldin joining in when he's around and contributing to recording and mixing. The sound is introspective, nostalgic Midwest emo, leaning emotional and honest, with a touch of country rock.

After a small DIY tour, panhandle. has opened for artists like Luke Borchelt and Slow Joy, and played shows across Oklahoma and Colorado, including Tulsa and Denver venues such as Resonant Head and Skylark Lounge. Recent work includes "Spiraling", recorded at Cardinal Song Studios, a song built around late-night nostalgia and the spiraling weight of memory.

Looking ahead, the project is focused on continued recording, releasing new music, and getting back on the road—maybe even Seattle, with a couch to crash on and his grandma in the crowd.
