Cryhard is an indie/alternative band based out of Oklahoma City and consists of members Bailey Little, Patrick Hanscom, Zachary Kampf, Jenny Mayes, and Malcolm Bensinger. Cryhard is a project in which everyone fills different roles depending on the track. This project has been in the works since 2018 and has had assistance from several good friends along the way.

Of Course I Will is the band's first full-length LP that reflects on themes of growing up, anxiety, and love. Recorded in Bailey’s spare bedroom, this album embraces its DIY roots with a warm lo-fi sound.
