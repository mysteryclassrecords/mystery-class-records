Cryhard's freshman album, Of Course I Will, now has its first-ever vinyl run. Featuring a beautiful sleeve, insert, and black vinyl. OCIW set them on a new path toward their now alt-rock, Midwest, and shoegaze sound. Get your first-edition copy while you can!

Of Course I Will is the band's first full-length LP, reflecting on themes of growing up, anxiety, and love. Recorded in Bailey's spare bedroom, the album embraces its DIY roots with a warm, lo-fi sound.
