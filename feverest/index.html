Feverest

Tulsa, OK

Feverest formed in Tulsa in the aftermath of the pandemic, when longtime friends from the local music scene finally aligned to start something new. With roots in multiple past projects, the band came together naturally through shared bills, friendships, and a desire to build something heavier and more immersive. Drawing from shoegaze, indie, and experimental influences, their music is dense, dreamy, and emotionally charged—balancing atmosphere with cathartic weight.

They released their debut EP, Blurred, in 2024, quickly building momentum through sold-out shows, festival appearances like Pug Fest, and shared stages with bands such as Glixen, Glare, Keep, and Leaving Time. Known for layered guitars, explosive dynamic shifts, and a visceral live presence, Feverest has established itself as one of Tulsa's most compelling new heavy-leaning projects.

Looking ahead, the band is focused on releasing new material through Mystery Class Records, taking their sound on the road, and continuing to evolve while staying true to the emotional core that brought them together.
