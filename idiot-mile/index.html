Idiot Mile

Oklahoma City, OK

Idiot Mile began as Pillow Head, formed by Ethan, Zach, and Henry, before Henry stepped away and Austin joined, shaping the lineup into what it is today. Built on loud guitars, the band blends slowcore and shoegaze with a strain of '90s nostalgia that still feels firmly rooted in the present. Songs like "Call Me," written by Austin before even joining the band, became some of their earliest releases and helped define the band's sound early on.

They’ve played local venues and DIY shows, released music through Mystery Class Records, and had their work featured on KATT 100.5 FM.

Idiot Mile is currently focused on finishing their debut album and taking the band on the road.
