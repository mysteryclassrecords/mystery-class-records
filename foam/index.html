Foam

Oklahoma City, OK

Foam is three friends from the emo and pop-punk scene who reunited after years apart to make loud, full shoegaze with a '90s/2000s twist. Drawing from early-2000s emo and '90s grunge, the band channels familiar influences into something heavy, melodic, and their own.

Their debut single, "Bind," explores the feeling of being trapped and the release that comes with breaking free, setting the tone for their first EP recorded in-house with Mystery Class Records. While Foam is a three-piece, their sound is anything but small, shaped by shared history, instinct, and giving each member space to shine.

With over a decade of collective experience in the Oklahoma music scene, Foam continues to play local shows, write music they're proud of, and steadily carve out their place in Oklahoma City.
